# 💼 Monetization Ideas

You can use this template in free or paid ways.

## Common business models

### 1. Public template + paid services
Keep the base repository public and charge for:
- custom agent builds
- setup and implementation
- integrations
- support
- consulting

### 2. Open core
Offer a free base version and paid premium packs.
Examples:
- advanced prompt packs
- vertical-specific toolkits
- enterprise governance add-ons
- deployment packs

### 3. Private premium repositories
Keep enhanced versions private and give paying clients access.

### 4. Productized services
Turn the template into offers like:
- done-for-you AI agent setup
- team onboarding
- premium internal templates
- AI operations retainers

## Important note
MIT allows broad reuse. If you want stricter commercial control, review your license strategy before building a business around exclusivity.
